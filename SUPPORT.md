## Self-Help Checklist

Before opening an issue:

1. Check the [FAQ](https://silentspike.github.io/complianceos/referenz/faq/)
2. Verify your Docker version: `docker --version` (19.03+)
3. Check health: `curl http://localhost:8001/api/health`
4. Review logs: `docker compose logs complianceos`
